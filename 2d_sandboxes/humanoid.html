Joints and links can be moved by dragging them with the mouse, or by using the mouse wheel while hovering them.

Informations

The inverse kinematics is only applied when a link is moved (dragged with the mouse), not when a joint is rotated.

See Inverse kinematics (IK) for a planar robot manipulator for more information about inverse kinematics, and Center of mass for details about the center of mass of an humanoid robot.
