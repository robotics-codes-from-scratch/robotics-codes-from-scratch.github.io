Challenge: Move the cube

Task

Grab the red cube on the orange box and move it on top of the green one. The cube must stay on the green box for 5 seconds to complete the challenge.

API

The following functions are available:

  • logmap(f, f0): Logarithmic map for $\mathbb{R}^3 \times \mathcal{S}^3$ manifold (with $e$ in tangent space)
  • logmap_S3(f, f0): Logarithmic map for $\mathcal{S}^3$ manifold (with $e$ in tangent space)
  • openGripper(index)
  • closeGripper(index)
  • isGripperOpen(index)
  • isGripperClosed(index)
  • isGripperHoldingSomeObject(index)

with index being 0 for the right arm and 1 for the left one.

Additionally, some variables are available:

  • cube_start_pos: Position of the red cube at the beginning [px, py, pz]
  • cube_start_orient_right: Suggested orientation to use to grab the red cube with the right arm [qx, qy, qz, qw]
  • cube_start_orient_left: Suggested orientation to use to grab the red cube with the left arm [qx, qy, qz, qw]
  • target_pos: Position of the top of the green box [px, py, pz]
  • target_orient_right: Suggested target orientation to use to move the red cube with the right arm [qx, qy, qz, qw]
  • target_orient_left: Suggested target orientation to use to move the red cube with the left arm [qx, qy, qz, qw]
  • right_chain: Kinematic chain of the right arm (see below)
  • left_chain: Kinematic chain of the left arm (see below)

Kinematic chains have the following methods and properties:

  • chain.fkin(x): Forward kinematics
  • chain.Jkin(x): Jacobian computation
  • chain.actuators: List of the indices of the joints that are part of the kinematic chain
Control

Click on the green run button to execute the code.

To rotate the camera, press the left mouse button and drag the mouse. The right mouse button let you translate the camera. The mouse wheel let you zoom in/out.
